Developed a strategic performance dashboard based on the Balanced Scorecard framework to monitor KPIs across Financial, Customer, Internal Process, and Learning & Growth perspectives.

**What I did:**
- Designed KPI structure aligned with business objectives
- Built ETL workflows using Power Query
- Created custom DAX measures for performance tracking
- Visualized insights in Power BI for executive-level decision making

**Tools:** Power BI, Power Query, DAX, Excel  
**Focus Area:** Business Performance, Data Analytics, Strategy
